# Glamping Checklist

## Essentials

- jacket, for clothing bring layers
- hat & gloves (it gets cold at night)
- trail shoes or hiking boots
- flip-flops or sandals (makes it easier to get to/from shower)
- iPhone / camera & charger
- sunglasses
- pillow (if you want to bring your own)
- travel kit (toothbrush, etc)

## Entertainment

- USB drive w/ movies (if you want something to watch on TV)
- DVD (no blueray)
- music on iPod / iPhone

## Food

- coffee
- trail mix
- granola bars
- oatmeal
- tater-tots & wafflemaker!
- rice, quinoa, veggies, etc.
- wine, beer, liquor
- bottled water
